Data Removal
At Wemon, we take data protection and user requests very seriously. We are committed to
ensuring that your personal information is handled with the utmost care and respect. Our
policies are designed to provide transparency and security, ensuring that you have full
control over your data. This document outlines the steps for requesting the deletion of your
account, as well as the specifics of our data deletion policy.
Steps to Request Account Deletion
To ensure a clear and transparent process for account deletion, we have simplified the steps
you need to follow:
Log into Wemon:
Open the Wemon app on your device and ensure you are logged in with your account.
Go to settings:
Go to the settings menu from the profile page.
Open account settings:
Look for the 'Account' option within the settings menu.
Delete account:
Press the ‘Delete account’ button and confirm the operation to delete your account.
Data Deletion Policy
At Wemon, we are committed to your privacy and data security. When you request the
deletion of your account, all your data will be permanently removed from our systems without
exception. This includes:
Personal Information:
Name, email address, phone number, and any other personally identifiable information.
Usage History:
Activity logs, interactions within the app, and any data related to the use of the application.
User-Generated Content:
Photos, videos, posts, comments, and any content you have uploaded or shared within the
application.
